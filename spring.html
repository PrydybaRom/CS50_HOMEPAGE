Peonies

The Chinese variety is the most popular type of peony. It produces large, pink or white flowers. They are characteristic for their scent - it can be smelled from afar. The plant typically blooms in May, but it might extend till the end of June - depending on the care, nutrition and weather conditions.

Snowdrop

Among the melting snow, one of the first harbingers of spring breaks through the ground - a snowdrop. Snowdrop is a beautiful low bulbous plant. Mostly snowdrops have blue flowers. However, there are species and hybrid varieties with white, pink and purple flowers. Depending on the type of snowdrop, it blooms in spring or autumn.
