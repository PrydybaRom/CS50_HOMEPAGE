Chrysanthemum

Chrysanthemum is a genus of flowering plants from the aster family. It consists of almost 40 species growing in Eurasia and in the north of North America. There are also many decorative varieties of chrysanthemums.

Dahlia

Dahlia flowers of the aster family are one of the most beautiful and long-blooming garden flowers flowers, represented by the widest palette of colors and a variety of forms. They bloom from July to frost, and this is the most important advantage of dahlias, apart from beauty. The first dahlia tubers were brought to Europe from Mexico at the end of the 18th century.

Gladiolus

The gladiolus flower is a favorite summer-autumn flower that decorates with different colors, our flower beds and gardens. To get abundant flowering of gladiolus, you need to know when to plant and digging gladiolus bulbs, and how to store them until next year. This plant belongs to bulbous bulbs, from the iris family. In the natural environment gladioli grow in the south, in the subtropics of Africa and southern Europe.
