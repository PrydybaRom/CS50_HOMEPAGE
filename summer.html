Summer flovers


Daisies

Daisies - no less symbolically decorate Ukrainian homes with their white and lush inflorescences. They look good in combination with other garden flowers, especially roses. In addition, and have useful properties - these are field daisies. But the cultivation of garden chamomile has become much more popular among amateur flower growers.

Lavender

Lavender is exactly the flower that, in addition to beauty in the garden, will also provide a strong aroma that will attract with its unique tastes of honey collectors. After all, it is valued for this, although in beauty with other flowers of course he can't compete. Lavender is grown in small quantities, usually in single bushes, without different compositions.

Peony

Peonies - it can be said without a mistake that they are one of the most popular and recognizable flowers in flower gardens, because of the large and lush flowers that rise above the others. Tree peony has found special distribution in horticulture. The plant is believed to be capricious, but she is worth it!
